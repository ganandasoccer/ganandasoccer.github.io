The Gananda Bandits will be hosting the 14th Annual Indoor Soccer Tournament on **Saturday March 2nd and Sunday March 3rd**. The round robin tournament will take place at the Ruben A. Cirillo High School and Gananda Middle School. An entry fee and roster is due by February 22, 2019. All teams are guaranteed three games. All players winning championship games will receive tourney t-shirts.

Refer to the [Tournament Packet]({{ site.url }}/download/Gananda Soccer Tournament Packet 2019.pdf) here for complete tournament information, registration and payment instructions.
